Detailed Description

A text which can be drawn.

The letters, TTF_Text and letterAtlas hold the textual data and the other attributes are used to create that data. To manipulate specific attributes unique for texts use 'SDL3_ttf'.

Texts share their instance with an object but many attributes will not be used for texts.

Every letter of the text will be drawn as an instance of the texture atlas.

Texts are part of the TextDevice and are referenced by transferbuffers and fonts.

See also
TextDevice, CreateText(), DeleteText(), SetText(), SetTextAttributes(), GetText()
